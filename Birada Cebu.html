Cebu is an island in the central Philippines, known for its rich Spanish colonial history and cultural heritage. It is the largest island in the Visayas region and contains several major cities. Cebu City, located on the eastern coast of Cebu Island, is one of the country’s largest cities and a bustling port. The island is also famous for its world-class beach resorts and diving spots.
